Fixed the folding of out-of-bounds ``rzz`` angles during transpilation. The backend's
target now declares ``angle_bounds`` of ``(0, pi/2)`` for the ``rzz`` gate, and
registers an ``rzz`` wrapper with the transpiler's ``WrapAngles`` pass, so that
``rzz`` gates with angles outside ``[0, pi/2]`` are folded into the supported range.
This mechanism replaces the usage of the ``IBMDynamicFractionalTranslationPlugin``
plugin and the :class:`.FoldRzzAngle` transpiler pass.
